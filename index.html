In this design challenge, I improved the Department of Labor (DOL)'s web branding and usability challenges observed during research.

Role:

  • UX Researcher
  • UI Designer

Responsibilities:

  • User research and analysis
  • Information architecture
  • Wireframing and prototyping
  • Usability testing

Timeline:

  • Feb - Mar 2022 (4-week sprint)

Problem

The U.S. Department of Labor (DOL) website provides information-rich resources to help all labor force participants and associated advocates. Popular, vital information visitors may need prompt access to is significantly challenging as the site is content-dense, and inconsistent navigation can complicate instead of alleviate the experience.

Solution

Leverage DOL's existing content to create a responsive web redesign that enables simple and functional content discovery.
